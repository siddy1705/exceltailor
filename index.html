Welcome to Excel Tailor Designs

Excel Men's Wear will, with the use of leading edge e-commerce solutions and a value offer, make online shopping of high quality custom wear as well as other clothes and accessories convenient, secure and fun. Code of conduct It is our objective to provide returns for our shareholders in a socially and environmentally responsible manner. We are committed to minimizing the environmental impact of our operations and our products through the adoption of sustainable practices and continuous improvement in environmental performance. As a minimum, we comply with the relevant environmental laws in the local community we operate. We are committed to respecting ethical standards and local regulations when it comes to minimum salaries, working conditions and social security in all our work locations. We expect our suppliers to work with us in meeting our environmental and social objectives. For our key suppliers of goods and services environmental requirements are an integral part of new contracts and contract reviews. We are, to the extent possible, monitoring the performance of our sub contractors and will only maintain cooperation with contractors who are performing in alignment with our code of conduct.
